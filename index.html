Frequently Asked Questions

How does AirBUS escrow work? ▼
When you book, your payment goes into AirBUS Escrow — a secure holding account. Funds are only released to the host 24 hours after your confirmed check-in. If there's an issue, our dispute team holds funds until resolved.
What is Lipa Mdogo? ▼
Lipa Mdogo lets you split your total booking cost into 4 equal installments at 0% interest. Pay the first installment to confirm your booking, with remaining payments spread over 30 days. No credit check required.
Are properties verified? ▼
Yes. Every property goes through our 3-step verification: host identity check, property inspection (remote or on-site), and listing accuracy audit. Properties with Platinum badge have undergone our highest-level certification.
How do I book AirChef or AirRide? ▼
Add services to any booking from the property page, or book independently from the Services menu. AirChef, AirRide, AirRelax, and AirDuka are available in all 23 countries. Payment is also escrow-protected.
What if the property doesn't match the listing? ▼
Guest Shield guarantees your protection. Report within 2 hours of arrival with photos, and we will rebook you at equal or better property at no extra cost, or issue a full refund within 24 hours.
How do hosts receive payment? ▼
Hosts receive payment 24 hours after guest check-in, via M-Pesa, bank transfer, or PayPal. AirBUS deducts a 8% platform commission. Platinum hosts enjoy reduced 5% commission rates.
